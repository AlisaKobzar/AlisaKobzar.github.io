Swap Space

April 18, 2022

Research assistant of the artistic research project SwapSpace.

Swap Space is a pilot project (spring 2022) at the University of Music and Performing Arts Graz that focused on novel forms of collaborative artistic research in which otherness, difference and distance between the participants are central and are brought into a cohesive form via the concept of the spatial.
